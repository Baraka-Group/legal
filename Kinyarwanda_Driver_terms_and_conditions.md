# AMATEGEKO N'AMATEGEKO KU BASHOFERI

**Yahinduwe bwa nyuma: 18 Werurwe 2026**

## MURAKAZE MURI Baraka GO

Aya Mabwiriza n'Amabwiriza ku Bashoferi ("Amabwiriza") agenga ikoreshwa rya porogaramu ya Baraka GO igendanwa na serivisi zijyanye nayo ("Serivisi") nk'Umushoferi uyoborwa na Baraka Group Ltd ("twebwe", "twe", cyangwa "yacu").

Nyamuneka soma aya Mabwiriza witonze mbere yo gukoresha Serivisi yacu nk'Umushoferi.

**Mu gukoresha Serivisi nk'Umushoferi, wemera kugengwa n'aya Mabwiriza. Niba utemeranya n'igice icyo ari cyo cyose cy'aya Mabwiriza, ntushobora kwinjira muri Serivisi.**

---

## 1. IBISOBANURO

**1.1** "Serivisi" bivuga urubuga rwa Baraka GO, harimo porogaramu zigendanwa, imbuga za interineti, na serivisi zijyanye nayo.

**1.2** "Umushoferi" bivuga wowe, umuntu utanga serivisi zo gutwara abantu binyuze muri Serivisi.

**1.3** "Umushoferi" bivuga umuntu ukoresha Serivisi gusaba no kwakira serivisi zo gutwara abantu.

**1.4** "Serivisi zo gutwara abantu" bivuze gutwara abatwara abagenzi utanga ukoresheje moto.

**1.5** "Konti" bivuze konti yihariye y'umushoferi yashyiriweho kugira ngo ugere kuri serivisi yacu.

**1.6** "Akarere" bivuze Repubulika y'u Rwanda n'ibindi bihugu bya Afurika aho serivisi iboneka.

## 2. UBWEMEREWE N'IYANDIKISHWA RYA KONTI Y'UMUSHOFERI

### 2.1 Imyaka n'Ibisabwa n'amategeko
Ugomba kuba ufite nibura imyaka 18 kandi ufite uburenganzira bwo gutanga serivisi zo gutwara abantu mu karere kawe. Mu gushinga konti y'umushoferi, uba uhagarariye kandi wemeza ko wujuje ibi bisabwa.

### 2.2 Ibisabwa mu Kwiyandikisha ku Bashoferi
Kugira ngo wiyandikishe nk'Umushoferi, ugomba gutanga amakuru nyayo kandi yuzuye arimo:
- Amazina yose
- Aderesi imeri
- Nimero ya terefone
- Ijambo ry'ibanga ryizewe
- Amakuru y'uruhushya rwo gutwara ibinyabiziga yemewe (inomero, ubwoko, itariki ntarengwa, uburenganzira bwo gutanga)
- Nibura imodoka imwe yanditse ifite ibisobanuro byuzuye (inomero ya plaque, ikirango, icyitegererezo, ibara, ubushobozi bw'umugenzi)
- Inyandiko zo kwiyandikisha ku modoka
- Amakuru y'ubwishingizi yemewe (utanga, inomero ya polisi, itariki ntarengwa)

### 2.3 Kugenzura Konti
Twifitiye uburenganzira bwo kugenzura amakuru yose utanga. Dushobora guhagarika cyangwa guhagarika konti yawe niba amakuru agaragaye ko atari yo, ari uburiganya, cyangwa ayobya.

### 2.4 Igenzura ry'inyuma
Ushobora gusabwa kugenzura inyuma harimo:
- Kugenzura umwirondoro
- Kwemeza ko uruhushya rufite agaciro

Twifitiye uburenganzira bwo kongera kugenzura buri gihe.

### 2.5 Konti imwe kuri buri mushoferi
Buri mushoferi ashobora kugira konti imwe gusa ikora. Konti nyinshi z'umuntu umwe zirabujijwe kandi zishobora gutuma konti zose zihagarikwa cyangwa zihagarikwa.

## 3. IMITERERE Y'UMUKOZI W'ISHYIRAHAMWE RYIGENGA

### 3.1 Imiterere y'Umubano
**Uri rwiyemezamirimo wigenga, ntabwo uri umukozi, umuhagarariye, cyangwa uhagarariye Baraka GO**. Ufite uburenganzira busesuye ku bijyanye na:
- Niba, igihe, n'aho watanga serivisi zo gutwara abantu
- Amasaha angahe yo gukora
- Ni iyihe ngendo isaba kwemera cyangwa kwanga

### 3.2 Nta sano ku kazi
Aya masezerano ntabwo ashyiraho umubano w'akazi, ubufatanye, ubufatanye, cyangwa ikigo hagati yawe na Baraka GO. Ntabwo ugenerwa:
- Amafaranga y'akazi (ubwishingizi bw'ubuzima, ikiruhuko gihemberwa, amafaranga y'izabukuru)
- Ingwate z'umushahara muto
- Amafaranga y'amasaha y'inyongera
- Amafaranga y'abakozi
- Andi mafaranga y'akazi ajyanye n'akazi

### 3.3 Inshingano zawe
Nk'umucuruzi wigenga, ni wowe wenyine ushinzwe:
- Amafaranga yose ajyanye n'imikorere y'imodoka (lisansi, kubungabunga, gusana, ubwishingizi, kwiyandikisha)
- Kugena inzira zo gufatira no gupakurura imodoka
- Kubahiriza amategeko n'amabwiriza yose yo mu muhanda
- Kwishyura imisoro yose ijyanye n'ibyo winjiza
- Umutekano wawe n'uw'abatwara imodoka

## 4. IBISABWA KU BURYO BWO KUGIRA IMPAMVU Z'IMODOKA N'UBURENGANZIRA

### 4.1 Uruhushya rwo gutwara imodoka rwemewe
Ugomba kuba ufite kandi ugakomeza:
- Uruhushya rwo gutwara imodoka rwemewe rujyanye n'ubwoko bw'imodoka ukoresha
- Uruhushya rwo gutwara imodoka rudarangiye cyangwa ngo ruhagarikwe
- Uruhushya rutangwa n'ubuyobozi bwemewe mu Karere kawe

### 4.2 Ibisabwa ku modoka
Imodoka zose zanditswe kuri konti yawe zigomba:
- Kuba ari izawe cyangwa ugomba kuba ufite uburenganzira bwo kuzikoresha
- Kuba mu mutekano, Isuku, kandi ikwiriye umuhanda
- Uzuza imyaka n'ibipimo ngenderwaho (nk'uko bigenwa n'amategeko y'akarere)
- Kugira iyandikwa ryemewe kandi ufite uruhushya rukwiye
- Gutsinda igenzura ry'ibinyabiziga buri gihe uko bikenewe
- Huza amakuru yatanzwe mu gihe cyo kwiyandikisha

### 4.3 Ibisabwa ku bwishingizi
Ugomba kugira:
- Ubwishingizi bw'ibinyabiziga busesuye nk'uko biteganywa n'amategeko y'u Rwanda cyangwa amategeko y'akarere akurikizwa
- Ubwishingizi bwemewe kuri serivisi zo gutwara abantu mu bucuruzi
- Icyemezo cy'ubwishingizi kiboneka kugira ngo gisuzumwe igihe icyo ari cyo cyose

Kudakomeza ubwishingizi bwemewe bizatuma konti yawe yo gutwara ibinyabiziga ihagarikwa ako kanya.
### 4.4 Imodoka N'impushya Zinshi
- Ushobora kwandikisha imodoka nyinshi kuri konti yawe
- Ushobora kwandikisha impushya zo gutwara imodoka nyinshi ku bwoko butandukanye bw'imodoka
- Buri modoka igomba kuzuza ibisabwa byose
- Ugomba kugaragaza imodoka ukoresha ubu mu gutanga serivisi

### 4.5 Ubwoko bw'Imodoka
Serivisi ishyigikira ubu:
- **Moto (Moto):** Gutwara abagenzi umwe

## 5. KWEMERA NO GUTANGA IBIKORWA

### 5.1 Ubusabe bwo gutwara abagenzi
- Uzahabwa ubusabe bwo gutwara abagenzi bushingiye kuri:
- **Aho uherereye** (hafi y'Umugenzi)
- **Ubwoko bw'imodoka yawe** (buhuye n'ibyo Umugenzi yasabye)
- **Ubwoko bw'aho uhagaze** (bugenwa n'urwego rw'ifatabuguzi ryawe)
- Niba Umugenzi ari mu rwego rw'aho uhagaze kandi agasaba ubwoko bw'imodoka yawe, uzabona ubusabe bwe
- **Amafaranga y'ibiciro NTABWO agira ingaruka ku kuba wakira ubusabe** - abashoferi bose bujuje ibisabwa mu rwego rw'aho uhagaze reba ubusabe bwose
- Ubusabe bwo gutwara abagenzi burimo:
- Aho umugenzi azajya atwara abagenzi
- Aho ujya
- **Amafaranga y'ingendo Umugenzi atanga kwishyura**

- Nta nshingano ufite yo kwakira ubusabe ubwo aribwo bwose bw'ingendo
- Ushobora kwakira poromosiyo zemerwa nawe kandi ukanga izitemewe

### 5.2 Kwemera ingendo
Iyo uhisemo kwemera ubusabe bw'ingendo:
- Wemeye gutanga serivisi y'ingendo **Umugenzi yatanze**
- Ingendo zirafunze kandi ntizishobora guhinduka
- Ugomba gukora ibishoboka byose kugira ngo urangize urugendo
- Ugomba kugera aho uzafatira mu gihe gikwiye
- Ugomba kumenyesha Umugenzi niba uhuye n'ibitinda
- Ugomba gukurikiza inzira igaragara muri porogaramu cyangwa indi nzira ikwiye

### 5.3 Guhagarika
- Guhagarika kenshi nyuma yo kwemererwa bishobora gutuma habaho imburizi, amanota make, cyangwa konti ihagarikwa
- Impamvu zumvikana zo guhagarika ntabwo zizaba bihanishwa

### 5.4 Imyitwarire y'umwuga
Ugomba:
- Gufata abatwara imodoka bose mu buryo bw'icyubahiro n'ubupfura
- Gukomeza imyitwarire y'umwuga igihe cyose
- Kudakora imyitwarire iyo ari yo yose y'urugomo, iterabwoba, ihohoterwa, cyangwa idakwiriye
- Gukurikiza amategeko n'amabwiriza yose yo mu muhanda
- Kudatwara ibintu bitemewe cyangwa ibicuruzwa bicuruzwa by'ubucuruzi
- Kudakoresha serivisi mugihe wanyoye inzoga cyangwa ibiyobyabwenge
- Kubungabunga isuku y'imodoka no kuba imeze neza
- Gukurikiza ibyifuzo by'abatwara imodoka (ubushyuhe, ijwi ry'umuziki, nibindi)

### 5.5 Umutekano
- Umutekano wawe n'umutekano w'abatwara imodoka ni ingenzi cyane
- Ufite uburenganzira bwo kwanga serivisi niba wumva ufite umutekano
- Tanga raporo y'impanuka iyo ari yo yose y'umutekano ako kanya ukoresheje porogaramu
- Gukurikiza amabwiriza yose y'umutekano n'inama

## 7. INZU N'IBYISHYURWA

### 7.1 Ingendo zo mu igare ry'abatwara abagenzi
**Abatwara abagenzi bagena amafaranga y'ingendo biteguye kwishyura ku ngendo.**

- Iyo umugenzi asabye urugendo, bagaragaza amafaranga batanga
- Ureba icyifuzo cy'umugenzi mbere yo gufata icyemezo cyo kwemera
- Ufite uburenganzira busesuye bwo kwemera cyangwa kwanga bitewe nuko ubona ko ari byiza
- Amafaranga y'ingendo agaragara mu busabe bw'urugendo ni amafaranga yose umugenzi azishyura

### 7.2 Isuzuma ry'ingendo
Mu gihe usuzuma ubusabe bw'urugendo, tekereza kuri ibi bikurikira:
- Intera yo kugera aho ufatira
- Intera yo kugera aho ujya
- Igihe giteganyijwe
- Imiterere y'umuhanda
- Ikiguzi cya lisansi
- Imiterere y'ikirere
- Igihe cyawe n'imbaraga zawe
- Amafaranga akwiye kuri serivisi

**Ntabwo usabwa kwemera igiciro ubona ko kidakwiye.**

### 7.3 Amafaranga y'abatwara abagenzi
**Ugumana 100% by'igiciro umugenzi yatanze kandi nawe wabyemeye.**

- Baraka GO ntabwo yishyuza amafaranga ya komisiyo cyangwa serivisi ku ngendo
- Amafaranga ubona muri ubusabe bw'urugendo ni amafaranga yose uzabona
- Nta kugabanywa, nta mafaranga ahishe

**Urugero:**
- Ibiciro by'umugenzi: RWF 2.000
- Inyungu zawe: RWF 2.000 (100%)

Ibi bivuze ko buri faranga Umugenzi atanga rijya kuri wewe.

### 7.4 Gutunganya Kwishyura
- Inyungu zibarwa mu buryo nyabwo kuri buri rugendo rurangiye
- Inyungu zikorwa ku ngengabihe isanzwe (buri munsi, buri cyumweru, cyangwa nkuko byavuzwe)
- Inyungu zoherezwa kuri konti yawe ya banki cyangwa kuri konti yawe ya telefoni igendanwa cyangwa mu ntoki nk'amafaranga.
- Gutunganya Kwishyura bishobora gufata iminsi 1-3 y'akazi bitewe n'uburyo bwo kwishyura, cyane cyane niba umugenzi yishyura binyuze mu kohereza amafaranga muri banki.

### 7.5 Ifaranga
Ibiciro byose n'inyungu bibarwa mu mafaranga y'u Rwanda (RWF) cyangwa andi mafaranga yo mu gihugu nk'uko bikurikizwa mu Karere kawe.

### 7.6 Imisoro
- Ni wowe wenyine ushinzwe kwishyura imisoro yose isabwa ku nyungu zawe
- Baraka GO ntabwo ikuraho imisoro ku nyungu zawe
- Dushobora gutanga raporo z'inyungu kugira ngo zigufashe kubahiriza imisoro
- Ganira n'umuhanga mu by'imisoro ku bijyanye n'inshingano zawe zo kwishyura

### 7.7 Impinduka ku biciro by'ingendo
Kubera ko ibiciro by'ingendo bishyirwaho n'abatwara abagenzi kandi ukemera, impinduka ni gake cyane. Ariko, dushobora guhindura ibiciro mu gihe:
- Amakosa yo gutunganya uburyo bwo kwishyura
- Amakosa ya tekiniki ya sisitemu
- Ibikorwa by'uburiganya
- Gutandukana gukomeye mu nzira (ku bwumvikane bwa buri wese)

**Icyitonderwa:** Ntushobora gusaba kongerwa kw'ibiciro nyuma yo kwemera urugendo. Amafaranga ubona kandi wemeye ni yo nguzanyo uzabona (100%, nta kugabanywa).

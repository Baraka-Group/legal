## 4. REQUESTING AND TAKING RIDES

### 4.1 Requesting Rides

To request a ride:
- Specify your current location (pickup point)
- Specify your destination (drop-off point)
- Set your fare offer - enter the amount you are willing to pay and be fair.
- Review your request details
- Confirm your request

### 4.2 Ride Matching

Your request (including your fare offer, pickup location, and destination) is sent to all nearby Drivers who:
- Are online and available
- Have the vehicle type you requested
- Are within their visibility radius of your location (based on their subscription tier)

All eligible Drivers see your request - the fare amount does not affect who receives your request.

Drivers see your pickup location, destination, and the fare amount you're offering.

Each Driver individually decides whether your offer is acceptable to them.

You will be notified when a Driver accepts, including Driver details and estimated arrival time.

How visibility radius works: 

Currently, drivers can see requests within 150 meters around and away from them. 

This means more Drivers may see your request, but each decides independently whether to accept based on your offer.

### 4.3 Waiting for Pickup

- Be present at the designated pickup location at the scheduled time
- Provide accurate pickup location information
- Wait for the Driver to arrive
- Contact the Driver if you need to clarify the pickup location

### 4.4 During the Ride

You must:
- Treat the Driver with respect and courtesy
- Not bring items that are illegal, dangerous, or prohibited
- Follow Driver instructions regarding vehicle capacity and baggage
- Not cause damage to the Driver's vehicle
- Not ask Drivers to violate traffic laws or take unsafe actions
- Not engage in violent, threatening, harassing, or inappropriate behavior

### 4.5 Vehicle Types

The Service currently offers:
- Moto (Motorcycle): Single passenger transportation

Additional vehicle types may be added as the Service expands.

## 5. PRICING AND PAYMENT

### 5.1 Rider-Set Pricing

You set the fare amount you are willing to pay for your ride.

When requesting a ride, you specify how much you are offering to pay.

Your offer is shown to nearby Drivers along with your pickup and destination.

Drivers can choose to accept or decline your offer based on their own criteria.

The amount you offer is the amount you will pay if a Driver accepts.

### 5.2 Setting Your Offer

When setting your fare offer, consider:
- Distance to your destination
- Time of day and traffic conditions
- Vehicle type you're requesting
- Weather conditions
- Current demand in your area
- Fair compensation for the Driver's time and effort

Note: Setting a fair and reasonable offer increases the likelihood of Drivers accepting your ride request quickly.

### 5.3 No Fare Changes After Acceptance

Once a Driver accepts your ride request, the fare is locked.

The fare cannot be changed during or after the ride.

You pay exactly what you offered (no hidden fees, no commission, no surprises).

Drivers receive 100% of what you pay - Baraka GO does not take any commission from rides.

Significant route deviations may be subject to review.

### 5.4 Currency

All fares are quoted in Rwandan Francs (RWF) or other local currency as applicable in your Territory.

### 5.5 Payment Methods

You can pay using:
- Mobile money (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, etc.)
- Cash (where available and at Driver's discretion)
- Bank transfer

### 5.6 Payment Processing

Payment is due immediately upon completion of the ride.

Use the payment method you specified while requesting the ride to pay the driver.

For any payments (cash, mobile money, or bank transfer), pay the Driver the exact amount you specified as shown in the app.

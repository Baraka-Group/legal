## INTANGIRIRO

Baraka Group Ltd yiyemeje kurinda ubuzima bwite n'umutekano w'amakuru yawe bwite. Iyi Politiki y'ibanga isobanura uburyo dukusanya, dukoresha, dutangaza, kandi tukarinda amakuru yawe iyo ukoresheje porogaramu yacu ya Baraka GO igendanwa na serivisi zijyanye nayo ("Serivisi").

Iyi Politiki y'ibanga ireba abakoresha bose ba Serivisi yacu, harimo n'abagenzi (abagenzi) n'abashoferi, muri Repubulika y'u Rwanda no mu bindi bihugu bya Afurika aho Serivisi yacu ikorera.

**Mu gukoresha Serivisi yacu, wemera uburyo amakuru avugwa muri iyi Politiki y'ibanga. Niba utemeranya n'ibikubiye muri iyi Politiki y'ibanga, nyamuneka ntukoreshe iyi Serivisi.**

Twubahiriza Itegeko N° 058/2021 ryo ku wa 13/10/2021 rigenga kurengera amakuru bwite n'ubuzima bwite mu Rwanda hamwe n'andi mategeko agenga kurengera amakuru mu turere dukoreramo.

## 1. AMAKURU DUKURIKIRANA

Dukusanya ubwoko butandukanye bw'amakuru aturutse ku bakoresha serivisi zacu n'ayerekeye, harimo:

### 1.1 Amakuru bwite Utanga

**Amakuru yo Kwiyandikisha kuri Konti:**
- Amazina yose
- Aderesi imeri
- Nimero ya terefone
- Ijambobanga (ribikwa mu buryo buhishe)
- Ishusho y'umwirondoro (ni ngombwa)

**Ku bashoferi, Byongeyeho:**
- Nimero y'uruhushya rwo gutwara ibinyabiziga n'ubwoko bwarwo
- Itariki ntarengwa y'uruhushya n'uburenganzira bwo gutanga
- Amakuru y'imodoka (nimero ya plaque, ikirango, icyitegererezo, ibara, ubushobozi bw'abagenzi)
- Inyandiko zo kwiyandikisha ku modoka
- Amakuru y'ubwishingizi (umutanga serivisi, nimero ya polisi, itariki ntarengwa yo kurangira)

**Itumanaho:**
- Amakuru utanga iyo uhamagaye serivisi ishinzwe abakiriya
- Ibitekerezo, amanota, n'isuzuma

### 1.2 Amakuru akusanywa mu buryo bwikora

**Amakuru y'aho uherereye:**
- **Ku bagenzi:** Aho uherereye nyaho iyo usabye urugendo, mu gihe cy'urugendo rukora, n'igihe porogaramu irimo gukoreshwa
- **Ku bashoferi:** Aho uherereye nyaho iyo uri kuri interineti kandi irahari, mu gihe cy'ingendo, no mu gihe runaka nyuma yo kurangiza ingendo
- Aho gutwara no gusohora ingendo zose zarangiye
- Amakuru y'inzira na GPS coordinates

**Amakuru y'Ikoreshwa:**
- Ibiranga porogaramu ukoresha n'uburyo ubyikoresha
- Amapaji cyangwa ecran ureba
- Igihe wamaze ku mapaji cyangwa ecran
- Porogaramu yangiritse na raporo z'amakosa
- Amakuru y'imikorere

**Amakuru y'ingendo n'ibikorwa:**
- Ubusabe bw'ingendo (bwakiriwe, bwarangiye, cyangwa bwahagaritswe)
- Amateka y'ingendo (itariki, isaha, aho gutwara no gusohora imodoka, intera, igihe)
- Amafaranga y'ingendo (yashyizweho n'abagenzi kandi yemerwa n'abashoferi)
- Uburyo bwo kwishyura bwakoreshejwe
- Amakuru y'umushoferi n'imodoka ku ngendo zarangiye
- Amakuru y'umushoferi ku ngendo zarangiye
- Amanota n'isuzuma ryatanzwe kandi ryakiriwe

### 1.3 Amakuru aturutse ahandi

**Serivisi z'abandi bantu:**
- Amakuru aturuka ku mbuga nkoranyambaga niba uhisemo guhuza konti yawe y'imbuga nkoranyambaga
- Amakuru aturuka ku bafatanyabikorwa bacu b'ubucuruzi n'abatanga serivisi

**Abandi bakoresha:**
- Amanota n'isuzuma riturutse ku bandi bakoresha
- Raporo z'ibyabaye cyangwa kutubahiriza amategeko

### 1.4 Kuki n'Ikoranabuhanga Bisa

Dukoresha kuki, beacons zo kuri interineti, n'ikoranabuhanga nk'iryo ryo gukurikirana amakuru kugira ngo dukusanye amakuru yerekeye ibikorwa byawe byo gusura. Ibi bishobora kuba birimo:
- Kuki z'igihe (zirangira iyo ufunze porogaramu)
- Kuki zihoraho (ziguma kuri igikoresho cyawe)
- Kuki z'isesengura kugira ngo wumve uburyo abakoresha bakoresha Serivisi yacu

Ushobora kugenzura kuki ukoresheje igenamiterere ry'igikoresho cyawe, ariko guhagarika kuki bishobora kugabanya ubushobozi bwawe bwo gukoresha bimwe mu bintu bya Serivisi.

## 2. UKO DUKORESHA AMAKURU YAWE

Dukoresha amakuru dukusanya mu mpamvu zikurikira:

### 2.1 Gutanga no kunoza Serivisi

- **Koroshya ingendo:** Guhuza Abatwara Imodoka n'Abashoferi baboneka hashingiwe ku hantu, ubwoko bw'imodoka, n'umurongo w'isura y'Umushoferi
- **Gucunga konti:** Gushinga no kubungabunga konti yawe, kwemeza abakoresha
- **Ubufasha ku bakiriya:** Gusubiza ibibazo, gukemura ibibazo, no gutanga ubufasha
- **Kunoza serivisi:** Gusesengura imiterere y'ikoreshwa kugira ngo kunoze imikorere ya porogaramu n'uburambe bw'umukoresha
- **Guteza imbere imikorere:** Gutegura imikorere mishya na serivisi

### 2.2 Umutekano n'umutekano

- **Kugenzura umwirondoro:** Kwemeza umwirondoro w'Abashoferi n'Abatwara Imodoka
- **Gukumira uburiganya:** Gutahura no gukumira ibikorwa by'uburiganya, kwinjira mu buryo butemewe, n'ibindi bikorwa bitemewe
- **Iperereza ku byabaye:** Gusuzuma impanuka, amakimbirane, impanuka z'umutekano, no kutubahiriza Amategeko n'amabwiriza yacu
- **Gusubiza mu buryo bwihutirwa:** Gutanga amakuru ku nzego zihutirwa iyo bibaye ngombwa

### 2.3 Itumanaho

- **Ubutumwa bw'ibikorwa:** Kohereza ibyemeza ry'ingendo n'amakuru mashya kuri serivisi
- **Itumanaho ry'ubufasha ku bakiriya:** Gusubiza ibibazo byawe n'ibyifuzo byawe
- **Itumanaho ryo kwamamaza:** Kohereza ibiciro byo kwamamaza, amabaruwa, n'amakuru mashya kuri Serivisi yacu (ushobora guhitamo kwiyambura igihe icyo ari cyo cyose)
- **Itumanaho ryihuse:** Kohereza imenyesha ku bijyanye n'uko imodoka ihagaze, umushoferi ageze, n'andi makuru mashya y'ingenzi
- **Ubutumwa bwo muri porogaramu:** Koroshya itumanaho hagati y'abagenzi n'abashoferi ku bijyanye n'ingendo zikora

### 2.4 Amategeko n'Iyubahirizwa ry'amategeko

- **Inshingano z'amategeko:** Kubahiriza amategeko, amabwiriza, inzira z'amategeko, n'ibyifuzo bya leta
- **Iyubahirizwa ry'amategeko:** Gushyira mu bikorwa Amategeko n'amabwiriza yacu n'izindi politiki
- **Kurengera uburenganzira:** Kurinda uburenganzira, umutungo, n'umutekano wa Baraka GO, abakoresha bacu, n'abaturage
- **Gukemura amakimbirane:** Gukemura amakimbirane no gukemura ibirego

### 2.5 Isesengura n'Ubushakashatsi

- **Isesengura ry'imikoreshereze:** Gusobanukirwa uburyo abakoresha bakorana na Serivisi
- **Ubushakashatsi ku isoko:** Gukora ubushakashatsi n'ubushakashatsi kugira ngo tunoze ibyo dutanga
- **Ibipimo by'imikorere:** Gukurikirana no gusesengura imikorere ya serivisi
- **Isesengura ry'ibigezweho:** Kumenya imiterere n'ibigezweho mu ubwikorezi

### 2.6 Guhindura imiterere y'umuntu

- **Uburambe bujyanye n'ibyo ukoresha:** Guhindura uburambe bwa porogaramu hashingiwe ku byo ukunda n'amateka y'ikoreshwa ryawe
- **Ibyifuzo:** Gutanga ibitekerezo ku byo ujyamo, inzira, cyangwa serivisi hashingiwe ku bikorwa byawe
- **Kwamamaza ku ntego:** Gutanga poromosiyo n'ibikorwa bifatika (ubiherewe uburenganzira aho bikenewe)

## 3. UKO DUSANGIRA AMAKURU YAWE

Ntitugurisha amakuru yawe bwite ku bandi bantu. Ariko, dushobora gusangira amakuru yawe mu bihe bikurikira:

### 3.1 Ku bandi bakoresha

**Abagenzi bashobora kubona:**
- Izina ry'umushoferi, ishusho, n'amanota (bigiye kongerwaho vuba)
- Aho umushoferi aherereye mu gihe cy'urugendo rukora
- Amakuru y'aho umushoferi aherereye (nimero ya telefoni, yapfutse cyangwa binyuze mu guhamagara muri porogaramu cyangwa nk'uko bisanzwe)

**Abashoferi bashobora kubona:**
- Izina ry'umushoferi, ifoto, n'amanota (bigiye kongerwaho vuba)
- Aho umushoferi azajya n'aho azajya (igihe icyifuzo cy'urugendo cyoherejwe)
- **Ikiguzi cy'ingendo cy'umushoferi** (amafaranga Umushoferi yiteguye kwishyura)
- Amakuru y'umushoferi (nimero ya telefoni, yapfutse cyangwa binyuze mu guhamagara muri porogaramu, nyuma yo kwemererwa)

**Iyo urugendo rurangiye:**
- Abashoferi n'abashoferi bombi bashobora kubona amakuru y'ingendo, harimo aho gutwara n'aho bazana, inzira banyuzemo, ibiciro bemeranyijweho, n'amanota y'abandi

### 3.2 Ku batanga serivisi

Dusangira amakuru n'abatanga serivisi b'abandi bakora serivisi kuri mu izina ryacu, harimo:
- **Abatanga serivisi zo kubika amakuru mu bicu:** Kubika amakuru mu buryo bwizewe
- **Abatanga serivisi zo gusesengura:** Gusesengura ikoreshwa rya porogaramu n'imikorere yayo
- **Ibikoresho byo gufasha abakiriya:** Gutanga serivisi z'ubufasha
- **Abatanga serivisi zo kugenzura inyuma:** Gusuzuma abashoferi (aho bikenewe)
- **Abatanga serivisi za SMS na imeri:** Kohereza ubutumwa
- **Abatanga serivisi zo gushushanya no kugenzura:** Gutanga serivisi zo kumenya aho ibintu biherereye

Aba batanga serivisi bategetswe n'amasezerano kurinda amakuru yawe no kuyakoresha gusa ku ntego twavuze.

### 3.3 Ku mpamvu z'amategeko

Dushobora gutangaza amakuru yawe niba bisabwa n'amategeko cyangwa mu gusubiza ubusabe bwemewe n'inzego za leta, harimo:
- **Abashinzwe iyubahirizwa ry'amategeko:** Kubahiriza inshingano z'amategeko, amabwiriza y'urukiko, cyangwa ubusabe bwa leta
- **Inzira z'amategeko:** Ku bijyanye n'imanza, ubukemurampaka, cyangwa gukemura amakimbirane
- **Umutekano n'umutekano:** Kurengera uburenganzira, umutungo, n'umutekano wa Baraka GO, abakoresha bacu, cyangwa rubanda
- **Ibibazo byihutirwa:** Gukemura ibibazo byihutirwa bibangamira umutekano w'umuntu uwo ari we wese.

### 3.4 Kwimura ubucuruzi

Niba Baraka GO ifite uruhare mu ihuzwa ry’ubucuruzi, kugura, kugurisha imitungo, guhomba, cyangwa ikindi gikorwa cy’ubucuruzi, amakuru yawe ashobora koherezwa nk'igice cy’icyo gikorwa. Tuzakubwira binyuze kuri imeri cyangwa itangazo rigaragara muri porogaramu ry’impinduka mu gutunga cyangwa gukoresha amakuru yawe bwite.

### 3.5 Ubiherewe uburenganzira bwawe

Dushobora gusangiza amakuru yawe n'abandi bantu mu gihe waduhaye uburenganzira bwawe bwo kubikora, nko:
- Gusangira amakuru y'urugendo n'abantu bo mu gihe cy'impanuka utanze
- Gusangira aho uherereye n'inshuti cyangwa umuryango binyuze mu buryo bwo gusangira urugendo
- Guhuza konti yawe na serivisi z'abandi bantu (urugero, imbuga nkoranyambaga)

### 3.6 Amakuru yakusanyijwe hamwe n'ayatamenyekanye

Dushobora gusangiza amakuru yakusanyijwe hamwe, atamenyekanye, cyangwa atamenyekanye adashobora gukoreshwa mu kukumenya, nko:
- Imibare yerekeye ikoreshwa rya porogaramu
- Imiterere y'ubwikorezi n'imiterere y'ibikenewe
- Amakuru rusange y'abaturage
- Ibipimo ngenderwaho by'imikorere

## 4. AMAKURU Y'AHO ARI

### 4.1 Gukusanya amakuru y'aho uherereye

Amakuru y'aho uherereye ni ingenzi mu mikorere ya serivisi yacu:

**Ku bagenzi:**
- Dukusanya aho uherereye nyaho iyo ufunguye porogaramu kugira ngo tukwereke abashoferi bari hafi yawe
- Dukusanya aho uherereye buri gihe mu gihe cy'urugendo rukora
- Gukusanya aho uherereye birahagarara iyo udakoresheje porogaramu (keretse wabifunguye Aho uherereye inyuma ku bintu byihariye)

**Ku bashoferi:**
- Duhora dukusanya aho uherereye neza iyo uri kuri interineti kandi uhari kugira ngo wemere ingendo
- Gukurikirana aho uherereye bikomeza mu gihe cy'ingendo zikora
- Dushobora gukusanya amakuru y'aho uherereye mu gihe gito nyuma yo kurangiza urugendo
- Ugomba gukoresha serivisi z'aho uherereye inyuma kugira ngo ukoreshe porogaramu y'umushoferi neza

### 4.2 Gukoresha Amakuru y'aho uherereye

Dukoresha amakuru y'aho uherereye kugira ngo:
- Huza abashoferi n'abashoferi baboneka hafi hashingiwe ku hantu, ubwoko bw'imodoka, n'umurongo w'isura y'umushoferi (urwego rw'ifatabuguzi)
- Gutanga uburyo bwo gutwara no gusohora
- Kunoza serivisi n'imikorere myiza
- Kongera imikorere y'umutekano
- Gusesengura imiterere y'ibikenewe no kunoza aho umushoferi ahagaze

### 4.3 Gusangira Amakuru y'aho uherereye

- **Mu gihe cy'ingendo zikora:** Aho uherereye mu gihe nyacyo hasangiwe n'undi muntu (Umushoferi cyangwa Umushoferi)
- **Ku batanga serivisi:** Amakuru y'aho uherereye ashobora gusangizwa n'abatanga serivisi zo kugena no kugenzura
- **Ku bw'umutekano n'amategeko:** Nk'uko byasobanuwe mu Gice cya 3.3

### 4.4 Kubika Amakuru y'aho uherereye

- Amakuru y'aho uherereye abikwa mu gihe bikenewe kugira ngo utange Serivisi, ukemure amakimbirane, kandi wuzuze inshingano z'amategeko

- Amakuru y'amateka y'aho hantu abikwa mu buryo buhuriweho cyangwa butamenyekanye kugira ngo habeho isesengura n'ivugurura rya serivisi

### 4.5 Kugenzura Uruhushya rwo Gukoresha Aho Ahantu

Ushobora kugenzura uburenganzira bwo gukoresha aho hantu ukoresheje igenamiterere ry'igikoresho cyawe:
- **Emera gusa mugihe ukoresha porogaramu:** Aho hantu hakusanywa gusa mugihe porogaramu ifunguye
- **Emera buri gihe (Abashoferi):** Aho hantu hakusanywa buri gihe iyo uri kuri interineti
- **Emera:** Ntuzashobora gukoresha uburyo bwo gukoresha aho hantu bwa Serivisi

Icyitonderwa: Kubuza gukoresha aho hantu bizakubuza gukoresha uburyo bw'ingenzi bwa Serivisi.

## 5. UMUTEKANO W'AMAKURU

### 5.1 Ingamba z'Umutekano

Dushyira mu bikorwa ingamba zikwiye za tekiniki n'iz'ikigo kugira ngo turinde amakuru yawe bwite, harimo:

**Uburinzi bwa Tekiniki:**
- Guhisha amakuru mu nzira hakoreshejwe protocole za SSL/TLS
- Guhisha amakuru y'ibanga mu gihe cyo gushakisha
- Kubika ijambo ry'ibanga mu buryo buhamye hakoreshejwe uburyo busanzwe bwo kugenzura umutekano
- Igenzura ry'umutekano rihoraho n'isuzuma ry'ibibazo by'ingorabahizi
- Insinga z'umuriro n'uburyo bwo gutahura abinjira
- Kwemeza API mu buryo butekanye

**Uburinzi bw'ikigo:**
- Kugenzura uburyo abakozi babona amakuru yabo bwite ku buryo bakeneye kumenya
- Amahugurwa y'abakozi ku bijyanye no kurinda amakuru no kuyarinda
- Amasezerano y'ibanga n'abakozi n'abatanga serivisi
- Uburyo bwo guhangana n'impanuka
- Gusuzuma no kuvugurura politiki z'umutekano buri gihe

### 5.3 Umutekano wa Konti

**Inshingano zawe:**
- Bika ijambo ry'ibanga ryawe mu ibanga kandi nturisangize abandi
- Koresha ijambo ry'ibanga rikomeye kandi ridasanzwe
- Sohoka kuri konti yawe mugihe ukoresha ibikoresho bisangiwe
- Tumenyeshe ako kanya niba ukeka ko ufite uburenganzira bwo kwinjira muri konti yawe konti
- Komeza amakuru yawe yo guhamagara (imeri na nimero ya terefone) agezweho

### 5.4 Itangazo ry'ihohoterwa ry'amakuru

Mu gihe habayeho ihohoterwa ry'amakuru rishobora guteza akaga gakomeye ku burenganzira bwawe n'ubwisanzure bwawe, tuzabikumenyesha n'urwego rubishinzwe rubishinzwe nta gutinda gukabije, nk'uko bisabwa n'amategeko abigenga.

### 5.5 Nta mutekano wuzuye

Nubwo dushyira mu bikorwa ingamba zumvikana z'umutekano, nta buryo bwo kohereza amakuru kuri interineti cyangwa ububiko bw'ikoranabuhanga butekanye 100%. Ntidushobora kwemeza umutekano wuzuye w'amakuru yawe.

## 6. KUBIKA AMAKURU

### 6.1 Ibihe byo Kubika

Tubika amakuru yawe bwite igihe cyose bikenewe kugira ngo twuzuze intego zivugwa muri iyi Politiki y'ibanga, keretse igihe kirekire cyo kubika amakuru gisabwa cyangwa cyemejwe n'amategeko.

**Amakuru ya Konti:**
- Bibikwa mu gihe cyose konti yawe ikora ndetse n'igihe gikwiye nyuma yo kubahiriza inshingano z'amategeko
- Ushobora gusaba ko konti isibwa igihe icyo ari cyo cyose

**Amakuru y'urugendo n'amakuru y'ibikorwa:**
- Bibikwa mu gihe cy'imyaka 7 kubera impamvu z'ibaruramari, imisoro, n'amategeko
- Bishobora kubikwa igihe kirekire mu buryo butazwi cyangwa bukusanyirijwe hamwe

**Amakuru y'aho uherereye:**
- Amakuru y'aho uherereye yakusanyirijwe hamwe cyangwa atazwi ashobora kubikwa igihe kitazwi

**Itumanaho n'ubufasha:**
- Amatike y'ubufasha n'itumanaho bibikwa mu gihe cy'imyaka 2
- Bishobora kubikwa igihe kirekire niba bifitanye isano n'amakimbirane akomeje cyangwa ibibazo by'amategeko

**Amanota n'isuzuma:**
- Bibikwa mu gihe cyose konti y'umukoresha yasuzumwe cyangwa nk'uko bisabwa ku mpamvu z'ubuziranenge bwa serivisi

### 6.2 Gusiba amakuru

Iyo tutagikeneye amakuru yawe, cyangwa igihe usabye gusiba (hakurikijwe inshingano z'amategeko), tuza:
- Gusiba amakuru muri sisitemu zacu zikora
- Gusiba amakuru cyangwa kuyakusanya kugira ngo atazongera kukumenya
- Gusenya amakuru mu buryo bwizewe kopi

Amwe mu makuru ashobora kubikwa muri sisitemu zo kubika amakuru mu gihe gito mbere yo gusiba burundu.

## 7. UBURENGANZIRA BWAWE N'IBYO WAHITAMO

Ufite uburenganzira bumwe na bumwe ku bijyanye n'amakuru yawe bwite, hakurikijwe amategeko akurikizwa:

### 7.1 Kuyabona no Kwimura

- **Uburenganzira bwo kuyakoresha:** Ushobora gusaba kopi y'amakuru yawe bwite dufite kuri wewe
- **Kuyakoresha:** Ushobora gusaba amakuru yawe mu buryo busanzwe, bukoreshwa, bushobora gusomwa n'imashini

### 7.2 Gukosora no Kuvugurura

- **Uburenganzira bwo gukosora:** Ushobora kuvugurura cyangwa gukosora amakuru yawe bwite ukoresheje igenamiterere rya porogaramu cyangwa ukatubwira
- Turagushishikariza gukomeza amakuru yawe neza kandi ajyanye n'igihe

### 7.3 Gusiba

- **Uburenganzira bwo gusiba:** Ushobora gusaba gusiba konti yawe n'amakuru yawe bwite
- Gusiba konti bishobora gukorwa binyuze muri porogaramu cyangwa ukavugana n'ubufasha
- Icyitonderwa: Amakuru amwe ashobora gukenera kubikwa kubera impamvu z'amategeko, ibaruramari, cyangwa umutekano

### 7.4 Guhagarika no Kwanga

- **Uburenganzira bwo kubuza gutunganya:** Ushobora gusaba ko dushyiraho imipaka ku buryo dukoresha amakuru yawe muri bimwe ibintu
- **Uburenganzira bwo kwanga:** Ushobora kwanga gutunganya amakuru yawe ku mpamvu zimwe na zimwe, nko kwamamaza mu buryo butaziguye

### 7.5 Gukuraho uburenganzira

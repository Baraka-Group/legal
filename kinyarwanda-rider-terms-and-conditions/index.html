AMATEGEKO N'AMABWIRIZA KU BAGENZI

Yahinduwe bwa nyuma: 18 Werurwe 2026

MURAKAZE MURI Baraka GO

Aya Mategeko n'Amabwiriza ku Bagenzi ("Amabwiriza") agenga ikoreshwa rya porogaramu ya Baraka GO igendanwa na serivisi zijyanye nayo ("Serivisi") nk'Umugenzi uyoborwa na Baraka Group Ltd. ("twebwe", "twe", cyangwa "yacu").

Nyamuneka soma aya Mabwiriza witonze mbere yo gukoresha Serivisi yacu.

Mu gukoresha Serivisi nk'Umugenzi, wemera kugengwa n'aya Mabwiriza. Niba utemeranya n'igice icyo ari cyo cyose cy'aya Mabwiriza, ntushobora kubona Serivisi.

1. IBISOBANURO

1.1 "Serivisi" bivuga urubuga rwa Baraka GO, harimo porogaramu zigendanwa, imbuga za interineti, na serivisi zijyanye nayo.

1.2 "Umugenzi" bivuga wowe, umuntu ukoresha Serivisi gusaba no kwakira serivisi zo gutwara abantu.

1.3 "Umushoferi" bivuga umuntu utanga serivisi zo gutwara abantu binyuze muri Serivisi.

1.4 "Serivisi zo gutwara abantu" bivuze gutwara abashoferi n'abashoferi bakoresheje moto.

1.5 "Konti" bivuze konti yihariye y'umugenzi yashyiriweho kugira ngo ugere kuri serivisi yacu.

1.6 "Akarere" bivuze Repubulika y'u Rwanda n'ibindi bihugu bya Afurika aho serivisi iboneka.

2. UBWEMEREWE NO KWIYANDIKISHA KONTI YO GUTWARA UMUYOBORO

2.1 Ibisabwa ku myaka

Ugomba kuba ufite nibura imyaka 18 kugira ngo ufungure konti kandi ukoreshe serivisi yacu. Mu gufunguza konti, uba uhagarariye kandi wemeza ko ufite nibura imyaka 18.

2.2 Ibisabwa mu Kwiyandikisha ku Mugenzi

Kugira ngo wiyandikishe nk'Umugenzi, ugomba gutanga amakuru nyayo kandi yuzuye arimo:

  • Amazina yose
  • Aderesi imeri
  • Nimero ya terefone
  • Ijambo ry'ibanga ryizewe

2.3 Umutekano wa Konti

Ushinzwe:

  • Kubungabunga ibanga ry'ibyangombwa bya konti yawe
  • Ibikorwa byose bibera muri konti yawe
  • Kutumenyesha ako kanya niba hari uburenganzira bwo kwinjira cyangwa gukoresha konti yawe

2.4 Kwemeza Konti

Dufite uburenganzira bwo kugenzura amakuru yose utanga kandi dushobora guhagarika cyangwa guhagarika konti yawe niba amakuru agaragaye ko atari yo, ari uburiganya, cyangwa ayobya.

2.5 Konti imwe kuri buri Mugenzi

Buri Mugenzi ashobora kugira konti imwe gusa ikora. Konti nyinshi z'umuntu umwe zirabujijwe kandi zishobora gutuma konti zose zihagarikwa cyangwa zihagarikwa.

3. GUKORESHA SERIVISI

3.1 Uruhushya rwo Gukoresha

Bitewe n'uko wujuje aya Mabwiriza, tuguha uruhushya ruto, rudashingiye ku kwihitiramo, rudashingiye ku kwimura, rudashingiye ku kwimura, rudashingiye ku kwimura, rushobora kwimurwa, rushobora kwimurwa kugira ngo ugere kuri serivisi kandi uyikoreshe ku giti cyawe, bitari mu bucuruzi.

3.2 Serivisi Iboneka

Serivisi iboneka mu turere twagenewe serivisi mu Karere.

Dushobora guhindura, guhagarika, cyangwa guhagarika igice icyo ari cyo cyose cya Serivisi igihe icyo ari cyo cyose nta nteguza ibanje.

Serivisi Iboneka ishobora kugira ingaruka ku bintu birimo guhuza umuyoboro, guhuza ibikoresho, ikirere, n'uko umushoferi aboneka.

3.3 Imiterere ya Platform

Baraka GO ni urubuga rw'ikoranabuhanga ruhuza Abashoferi n'Abashoferi. Ntidutanga serivisi zo gutwara abantu ubwacu. Serivisi zo gutwara abantu zitangwa n'Abashoferi bigenga bakoresha uru rubuga.

4. GUSABA NO GUFATA INGENDO

4.1 Gusaba Ingendo

Kugira ngo usabe urugendo:

  • Garagaza aho uherereye ubu (aho uzajya)
  • Garagaza aho uzajya (aho uzajya)
  • Shyiraho ikiguzi cy'ingendo - andika amafaranga witeguye kwishyura kandi ube inyangamugayo.
  • Suzuma amakuru y'ubusabe bwawe
  • Emeza ubusabe bwawe

4.2 Guhuza Ingendo

Ubusabe bwawe (harimo ikiguzi cy'ingendo, aho uzajya, n'aho uzajya) bwoherezwa ku bashoferi bose ba hafi:

  • Bari kuri interineti kandi barahari
  • Bafite ubwoko bw'ikinyabiziga wasabye
  • Bari mu ntera bareba aho uherereye (hashingiwe ku rwego rw'ifatabuguzi ryabo)

Abashoferi bose bujuje ibisabwa babona ubusabe bwawe - amafaranga y'ingendo ntagira ingaruka ku bakira ubusabe bwawe.

Abashoferi babona aho uzajya, aho uzajya, n'amafaranga y'ingendo utanga.

Buri mushoferi yihitiramo ku giti cye niba ikiguzi cyawe cyemerwa na bo.

Uzamenyeshwa igihe umushoferi yemeye, harimo amakuru y'umushoferi n'igihe giteganijwe cyo kuhagera.

Uburyo umurongo w'ibanga ugaragara:

Ubu, abashoferi bashobora kubona ubusabe muri metero 150 hirya no hino no kure yabo.

Ibi bivuze ko abashoferi benshi bashobora kubona ubusabe bwawe, ariko buri wese yihitiramo ku giti cye niba yemera hashingiwe ku byo watanze.

4.3 Gutegereza Gutwara

  • Kuba uri aho wagenewe gufatira ikinyabiziga ku gihe cyagenwe
  • Tanga amakuru nyayo y'aho wafatira ikinyabiziga
  • Tegereza Umushoferi aje
  • Vugana n'Umushoferi niba ukeneye gusobanura neza aho wafatira ikinyabiziga

4.4 Mu gihe cy'urugendo

Ugomba:

  • Gufata Umushoferi mu buryo bw'icyubahiro n'ubupfura
  • Kutazana ibintu bitemewe n'amategeko, biteje akaga, cyangwa bibujijwe
  • Kurikiza amabwiriza y'Umushoferi yerekeye ubushobozi bw'ikinyabiziga n'imizigo
  • Kudatera kwangiza ikinyabiziga y'Umushoferi
  • Kudasaba Abashoferi kurenga ku mategeko y'umuhanda cyangwa gufata ingamba ziteje akaga
  • Kudakora imyitwarire y'urugomo, itera ubwoba, itoteza, cyangwa idakwiriye

4.5 Ubwoko bw'Ibinyabiziga

Serivisi itanga ubu:

  • Moto (Moto): Gutwara abagenzi umwe

Inyongera z'ubwoko bw'ibinyabiziga zishobora kongerwamo uko Serivisi igenda ikura.

5. IGICIRO N'IYISHYURWA

5.1 Igiciro cy'ikinyabiziga itwara abagenzi

Ushyiraho amafaranga y'ingendo witeguye kwishyura ku rugendo rwawe.

Mu gihe usaba urugendo, ugena amafaranga wishyura.

Itangwa ryawe ryerekanwa abashoferi ba hafi hamwe n'ikinyabiziga yawe n'aho ujya.

Abashoferi bashobora guhitamo kwemera cyangwa kwanga itangwa ryawe hashingiwe ku bipimo byabo.

Amafaranga utanga ni amafaranga uzishyura niba umushoferi yemeye.

5.2 Gushyiraho Itangwa ryawe

Mu gihe ushyiraho itangwa ryawe ry'ingendo, tekereza kuri ibi bikurikira:

  • Intera yo kugera aho ujya
  • Igihe cy'umunsi n'imiterere y'ikinyabiziga
  • Ubwoko bw'ikinyabiziga usaba
  • Imiterere y'ikirere
  • Ubusabe bw'ubu mu gace utuyemo
  • Amafaranga akwiye ku gihe cy'umushoferi n'imbaraga ze

Icyitonderwa: Gushyiraho itangwa ryiza kandi rikwiye byongera amahirwe y'abashoferi kwakira ubusabe bwawe bw'ingendo vuba.

5.3 Nta mpinduka z'ingendo nyuma yo kwemererwa

Iyo umushoferi yemeye ubusabe bwawe bw'ingendo, ikiguzi kirafungwa.

Igiciro ntigishobora guhindurwa mu gihe cy'urugendo cyangwa nyuma yarwo.

Wishyura neza icyo watanze (nta mafaranga ahishe, nta komisiyo, nta gutungurwa).

Abashoferi bahabwa 100% by'ibyo wishyura - Baraka GO nta komisiyo ifata mu ngendo.

Itandukaniro rikomeye mu nzira rishobora gusuzumwa.

5.4 Ifaranga

Ibiciro byose bitangwa mu mafaranga y'u Rwanda (RWF) cyangwa andi mafaranga yo mu gihugu nk'uko bikurikizwa mu Karere kawe.

5.5 Uburyo bwo kwishyura

Ushobora kwishyura ukoresheje:

  • Amafaranga ya telefoni (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, n'ibindi)
  • Amafaranga (aho aboneka kandi ku bushake bw'umushoferi)
  • Kohereza amafaranga kuri banki

5.6 Gutunganya kwishyura

Kwishyura bigomba guhita birangira urugendo.

Koresha uburyo bwo kwishyura wavuze mugihe usaba urugendo kwishyura umushoferi.

Ku bwishyu ubwo aribwo bwose (amafaranga ya telefoni, amafaranga ya telefoni, cyangwa kohereza amafaranga kuri banki), wishyure umushoferi amafaranga nyayo wavuze nk'uko bigaragara muri porogaramu.

5.8 Amakimbirane yo kwishyura

Kubera ko wishyiriyeho amafaranga y'ingendo, impaka zo kwishyura ni gake cyane. Ariko, niba wemera ko hari ikibazo:

  • Hamagara serivisi ishinzwe abakiriya ukoresheje porogaramu mu masaha 24 uhereye igihe urugendo rubereye
  • Tanga ibisobanuro birambuye (urugero: amafaranga yishyuwe atari yo, kwishyura byakozwe kabiri, nibindi)

Tuzasuzuma kandi dusubize mu minsi 3-5 y'akazi.

Gusubizwa amafaranga cyangwa impinduka (niba byemewe) bizashyirwa kuri konti yawe.

Icyitonderwa: Ntushobora kuvuguruza ibiciro kuko watanze amafaranga menshi cyane. Amafaranga utanga ni amafaranga wemera kwishyura niba umushoferi yemeye.

6. GUHAGARIKA

6.1 Guhagarika Mbere yuko Umushoferi Abyemera

Ushobora guhagarika ubusabe bw'urugendo mbere yuko Umushoferi yemera nta gihano.

6.2 Guhagarika Nyuma yuko Umushoferi Abyemera

Niba uhagaritse nyuma yuko Umushoferi yemeye kandi ari mu nzira igana aho uzajya:

  • Ushobora kwishyuzwa amafaranga yo guhagarika
  • Amafaranga yo guhagarika ahemba Umushoferi igihe cye n'intera yagiyemo
  • Amafaranga yo guhagarika agaragazwa muri porogaramu

6.3 Impamvu zumvikana zo guhagarika

Ntuzacibwa amafaranga yo guhagarika niba:

  • Umushoferi yatinze cyane kurenza igihe giteganyijwe cyo kuhagera
  • Umushoferi yanze kuza aho uzajya
  • Uhuye n'ikibazo cyihutirwa cyemewe
  • Hari impungenge z'umutekano

Kugira ngo usabe ko amafaranga yo guhagarika akurwaho, hamagara serivisi ishinzwe abakiriya kugira ngo ubahe ibisobanuro birambuye.

7. UMUTEKANO N'IMYITWARIRE

7.1 Umutekano Wawe

  • Tanga raporo y'ibibazo by'umutekano ako kanya ukoresheje porogaramu cyangwa serivisi zihutirwa

7.2 Serivisi zihutirwa

Mu gihe cy'impanuka:

  • Hamagara serivisi zihutirwa zo mu gace utuyemo ako kanya (Polisi: 112, Ubuvuzi: 912 mu Rwanda)
  • Tanga raporo ku bufasha bwa Baraka GO vuba bishoboka.

7.3 Raporo y'Ibibazo

Tanga raporo kuri ibi bikurikira ako kanya:

  • Imyitwarire idakwiye y'abashoferi
  • Ibibazo by'umutekano
  • Ivangura cyangwa ihohoterwa
  • Kwangiza imitungo

7.4 Imyitwarire ibujijwe

Wemeye kutabikora:

  • Gukoresha Serivisi mu buryo butemewe n'amategeko
  • Gutwara ibintu bitemewe cyangwa ibintu bya magendu
  • Gutoteza, gutera ubwoba, cyangwa guhohotera abashoferi
  • Gutandukanya abashoferi
  • Gutanga amakuru atari yo cyangwa uburyo bwo kwishyura butari bwo
  • Gukora konti nyinshi kugira ngo ukoreshe nabi poromosiyo
  • Guhindura amanota cyangwa isuzuma
  • Gusaba ingendo nta ntego yo kuzitwara
  • Gusangiza konti yawe n'abandi

8. IBINTU BYABUZE

8.1 Inshingano

Baraka GO ntabwo ari yo ishinzwe ibintu byabuze mu modoka. Ushinzwe kugenzura ko ufite ibintu byawe byose igihe usohoka mu modoka.

8.2 Kugarura Ibintu Byabuze

Niba watakaje ikintu:

  • Bitangaze ukoresheje porogaramu yabuze ikintu vuba bishoboka
  • Tanga ibisobanuro birambuye by'ikintu

Tuzakorohereza itumanaho hagati yawe n'umushoferi.

Umushoferi ntategetswe gusubiza ibintu ariko turabashishikariza ubufatanye.

8.3 Amafaranga yo kugaruza

Abashoferi bashobora kwishyuza amafaranga akwiye yo kugaruza ibintu byabuze (ku bw'igihe cyabo n'imbaraga zabo).

Amafaranga yose yo kugaruza agomba kumvikana hagati yawe n'umushoferi.

Baraka GO ntabwo ishyiraho cyangwa ngo yishyuze amafaranga yo kugaruza.

8.4 Ibintu by'agaciro

Turagusaba cyane:

  • Kutareka ibintu by'agaciro mu modoka nta byirinzwe
  • Kugenzura ikinyabiziga mbere yo gusohoka
  • Gufata inshingano ku bintu byawe bwite

9. IMYENDA YO KWAMAMAZA N'IGABANYUKA

9.1 Poromosiyo

Dushobora gutanga inguzanyo zo kwamamaza, kugabanyirizwa ibiciro, cyangwa bonus zo kohereza rimwe na rimwe.

9.2 Amategeko agenga Poromosiyo

Buri poromosiyo igengwa n'amategeko n'amabwiriza yihariye.

Poromosiyo ishobora kugira amatariki ntarengwa.

Poromosiyo ntishobora guhuzwa keretse bivuzwe neza.

Twifitiye uburenganzira bwo guhindura cyangwa guhagarika poromosiyo igihe icyo ari cyo cyose.

9.3 Porogaramu yo kohereza

Ushobora kubona inguzanyo wohereza abakoresha bashya.

Wowe n'umukoresha woherejwe mushobora kubona inguzanyo.

Inguzanyo zo kohereza zigengwa n'ibisabwa n'amategeko.

Poromosiyo z'uburiganya zizatera gutakaza inguzanyo no guhagarika konti.

9.4 Ikoreshwa ry'Inguzanyo

Inguzanyo zikoreshwa mu buryo bwikora ku ngendo zujuje ibisabwa.

Inguzanyo nta gaciro k'amafaranga zifite kandi ntizishobora koherezwa cyangwa kugurishwa.

Inguzanyo zitakoreshejwe zishobora kurangira hakurikijwe amabwiriza yo kwamamaza.

10. UBUBANGA BW'AMAKURU N'IKURIKIRANA AHANTU

10. Gukusanya amakuru y'aho aherereye

Kugira ngo ukoreshe Serivisi:

  • Ugomba guha porogaramu uburenganzira bwo kugera aho igikoresho cyawe giherereye
  • Dukusanya aho uherereye iyo usabye urugendo no mu gihe cy'ingendo
  • Amakuru y'aho aherereye akoreshwa mu koroshya gutwara, kugenda no kubara ibiciro
  • Aho uherereye hasangirwa n'Umushoferi mu gihe cy'ingendo

10.2 Politiki y'Ibanga

Ikoreshwa ryawe rya Serivisi rigengwa na Politiki yacu y'Ibanga, isobanura uburyo dukusanya, dukoresha, kandi tugasangira amakuru yawe. Nyamuneka suzuma Politiki yacu y'Ibanga witonze.

10.3 Uruhushya rwo Gukoresha Aho Uherereye

Ushobora kugenzura uburenganzira bwo gukoresha aho uherereye ukoresheje imiterere y'igikoresho cyawe:

  • "Emera gusa mugihe ukoresha porogaramu" birahagije ku batwara ikinyabiziga
  • Kubuza konti kwinjira bizakubuza gukoresha Serivisi

11.2 Guhagarika imirimo hakoreshejwe porogaramu ya Motor

11.3 Ingaruka zo guhagarika imirimo

Iyo uhagaritse imirimo:

13. GUKEMURA AMATAKA

13.1 Amategeko Agenga

Aya Mabwiriza azagengwa n'amategeko ya Repubulika y'u Rwanda, hatitawe ku ngingo z'amategeko avuguruzanya.

13.2 Ububasha

Amakimbirane yose agomba kugengwa n'ububasha bwihariye bw'inkiko za Kigali, mu Rwanda.

13.3 Kubanza Gushyikirana

Mu gihe habayeho amakimbirane, impande zemeranya kubanza kugerageza gukemura ikibazo binyuze mu biganiro byiza mu minsi mirongo itatu (30).

13.4 Ubukemurampaka (Ntabwo ari ngombwa)

14. INGINGO RUSANGE

14.1 Impinduka

Twifitiye uburenganzira bwo guhindura aya Mabwiriza igihe icyo ari cyo cyose. Tuzakubwira impinduka zikomeye binyuze mu:

  • Kohereza Amabwiriza mashya muri porogaramu
  • Kohereza imenyesha rya imeri
  • Kwerekana imenyesha rigaragara muri porogaramu

Gukomeza gukoresha nyuma y'impinduka bitanga uburenganzira bwo kwemerwa.

14.2 Gutandukanya

Iyo habonetse ingingo idakurikizwa, iyo ngingo igomba kugarukira ku rugero ruto rukenewe, kandi ingingo zisigaye zizakomeza gukoreshwa.

14.3 Nta gusiba

Kunanirwa gushyira mu bikorwa uburenganzira cyangwa ingingo iyo ari yo yose ntabwo bizaba ari ugusiba ubwo burenganzira cyangwa ingingo.

14.4 Inshingano

Ntushobora gutanga aya Mabwiriza tutabanje kubyemeranyaho mu nyandiko. Dushobora gutanga aya Mabwiriza nta mbogamizi.

14.5 Force Majeure

Ntituzaryozwa kunanirwa gukora bitewe n'ibintu tudashobora kugenzura, harimo ibiza, intambara, iterabwoba, icyorezo, imyigaragambyo, cyangwa ibikorwa bya leta.

14.6 Amasezerano yose

Aya Mabwiriza, hamwe na Politiki y'ibanga, bigize amasezerano yose hagati yawe na Baraka GO yerekeye Serivisi.

14.7 Kwaguka mu Karere

Uko Baraka GO igenda ikwirakwira mu bindi bihugu bya Afurika, aya Mabwiriza azavugururwa kugira ngo ahuze n'amategeko n'amabwiriza yo mu gace.

14.8 Ururimi

Aya Mabwiriza atangwa mu Cyongereza. Mu gihe habayeho amakimbirane hagati ya verisiyo y'Icyongereza n'impinduka iyo ari yo yose, verisiyo y'Icyongereza niyo izatsinda.

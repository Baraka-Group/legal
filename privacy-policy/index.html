INTRODUCTION

Baraka Group Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Baraka GO mobile application and related services (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our Service, including Riders (passengers) and Drivers, within the Republic of Rwanda and other African countries where our Service operates.

By using our Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

We comply with the Law N° 058/2021 of 13/10/2021 on the Protection of Personal Data and Privacy in Rwanda and other applicable data protection laws in the territories where we operate.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service, including:

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Email address

For Drivers, Additionally:

Communications:

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • For Riders: Your real-time precise location when you request a ride, during an active ride, and when the app is in use
  • For Drivers: Your continuous real-time precise location when you are online and available, during rides, and for a period after completing rides
  • Pickup and drop-off locations for all completed rides
  • Route information and GPS coordinates

Usage Information:

  • App features you use and how you use them

Ride and Transaction Information:

1.3 Information from Other Sources

Third-Party Services:

  • Information from social media platforms if you choose to connect your social media account
  • Information from our business partners and service providers

Other Users:

  • Ratings and reviews from other users
  • Reports of incidents or violations

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. This may include:

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

Riders can see:

  • Driver's name, image, and ratings (soon to be added)
  • Driver's real-time location during an active ride
  • Driver's contact information (phone number, masked or through in-app calling or as usual)

Drivers can see:

  • Rider's name, photo, and rating (soon to be added)
  • Rider's pickup location and destination (when ride request is sent)
  • Rider's fare offer (the amount Rider is willing to pay)
  • Rider's contact information (phone number, masked or through in-app calling, after acceptance)

After a ride is completed:

  • Both Riders and Drivers can see ride details, including pickup and drop-off locations, route taken, agreed-upon fare, and each other's ratings

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud storage providers: To store data securely
  • Analytics providers: To analyze app usage and performance
  • Customer support tools: To provide support services
  • Background check providers: To screen Drivers (where applicable)
  • SMS and email service providers: To send communications
  • Mapping and navigation providers: To provide location services

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, including:

  • Law enforcement: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Legal proceedings: In connection with litigation, arbitration, or dispute resolution
  • Safety and security: To protect the rights, property, and safety of Baraka GO, our users, or the public
  • Emergency situations: To respond to emergencies that threaten the safety of any person

3.4 Business Transfers

If Baraka GO is involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, bankruptcy, or other business transaction, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice in the app of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your explicit consent to do so, such as:

  • Sharing trip details with emergency contacts you designate
  • Sharing your location with friends or family through trip sharing features
  • Connecting your account with third-party services (e.g., social media)

3.6 Aggregated and De-identified Information

We may share aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you, such as:

  • Statistics about app usage
  • Transportation trends and demand patterns
  • General demographic information
  • Performance benchmarks

4. LOCATION DATA

4.1 Collection of Location Data

Location data is essential to the functioning of our Service:

For Riders:

  • We collect your precise location when you open the app to show you nearby drivers
  • We continuously collect your location during an active ride
  • Location collection stops when you are not actively using the app (unless you have enabled background location for specific features)

For Drivers:

  • We continuously collect your precise location when you are online and available to accept rides
  • Location tracking continues during active rides
  • We may collect location data for a short period after completing a ride
  • You must enable background location services to use the Driver app effectively

4.2 Use of Location Data

We use location data to:

  • Match Riders with nearby available Drivers based on location, vehicle type, and Driver visibility radius (subscription tier)
  • Provide pickup and drop-off navigation
  • Improve Service coverage and efficiency
  • Enhance safety features
  • Analyze demand patterns and optimize driver positioning

4.3 Sharing of Location Data

  • During active rides: Your real-time location is shared with the other party (Driver or Rider)
  • With service providers: Location data may be shared with mapping and navigation service providers
  • For safety and legal purposes: As described in Section 3.3

4.4 Location Data Retention

  • Active ride location data is retained for a period necessary to provide the Service, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations
  • Historical location data is retained in aggregated or anonymized form for analytics and service improvement

4.5 Controlling Location Permissions

You can control location permissions through your device settings:

  • Allow only while using the app: Location is collected only when the app is open
  • Allow always (Drivers): Location is collected continuously when you are online
  • Deny: You will not be able to use location-based features of the Service

Note: Denying location access will prevent you from using core features of the Service.

6. DATA RETENTION

6.1 Retention Periods

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Account Information:

  • Retained for the duration of your account plus a reasonable period afterwards to comply with legal obligations
  • You may request account deletion at any time

Ride History and Transaction Data:

  • Retained for up to 7 years for accounting, tax, and legal compliance purposes
  • May be retained longer in anonymized or aggregated form

Location Data:

  • Aggregated or anonymized location data may be retained indefinitely

Communications and Support:

  • Support tickets and communications are retained for up to 2 years
  • May be retained longer if related to ongoing disputes or legal matters

Ratings and Reviews:

  • Retained for the lifetime of the reviewed user's account or as required for service quality purposes

6.2 Deletion of Data

When we no longer need your information, or upon your request for deletion (subject to legal obligations), we will:

  • Delete the data from our active systems
  • Anonymize or aggregate the data so it can no longer identify you
  • Securely destroy physical copies

Some information may be retained in backup systems for a limited period before permanent deletion.

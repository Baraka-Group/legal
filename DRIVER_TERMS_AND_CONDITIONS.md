## 7. EARNINGS AND PAYMENTS

### 7.1 Rider-Set Fare Model
**Riders set the fare amount they are willing to pay for rides.**

- When a Rider requests a ride, they specify how much they are offering
- You see the Rider's offer before deciding whether to accept
- You have complete freedom to accept or decline based on whether you find the offer fair
- The fare shown in the ride request is the total amount the Rider will pay

### 7.2 Fare Evaluation
When evaluating a ride request, consider:
- Distance to pickup location
- Distance to destination
- Estimated time
- Traffic conditions
- Fuel costs
- Weather conditions
- Your time and effort
- Fair compensation for the service

**You are never required to accept an offer you find unfair.**

### 7.3 Driver Earnings
**You keep 100% of the fare the Rider offered and you accepted.**

- Baraka GO does not charge any commission or service fees on rides
- The amount you see in the ride request is the full amount you will earn
- No deductions, no hidden fees

**Example:**
- Rider offers: RWF 2,000
- Your earnings: RWF 2,000 (100%)

This means every franc the Rider offers goes directly to you.

### 7.4 Payment Processing
- Earnings are calculated in real-time for each completed ride
- Payments are processed on a regular schedule (daily, weekly, or as specified)
- Payments are transferred to your registered bank account or mobile money account or in-hand as in form of cash.
- Payment processing may take 1-3 business days depending on the payment method, especially if the rider pays via bank transfer.

### 7.5 Currency
All fares and earnings are calculated in Rwandan Francs (RWF) or other local currency as applicable in your Territory.

### 7.6 Taxes
- You are solely responsible for payment of all applicable taxes on your earnings
- Baraka GO does not withhold taxes from your earnings
- We may provide earnings reports to assist with tax compliance
- Consult with a tax professional regarding your tax obligations

### 7.7 Fare Adjustments
Since fares are set by Riders and accepted by you, adjustments are rare. However, we may adjust fares in cases of:
- Payment processing errors
- System technical errors
- Fraudulent activity
- Significant route deviations (with mutual agreement)

**Note:** You cannot request fare increases after accepting a ride. The amount you see and accept is exactly the amount you will earn (100%, no deductions).
